**How to Use:**
1. Click "MIDI Channel Detect" and move a control to identify it
2. Select that channel/control in a mapping row
3. Enter your function call using `{VALUE}` as the placeholder
4. Set your min/max values
5. Click "Save Configuration" to persist your settings

The MIDI values (0-127) will be automatically scaled to your specified min/max range and executed in real-time.
